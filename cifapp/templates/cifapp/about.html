The main contributors

Junlei Qi, is a postdoc at the School of Materials Science and Engineering, Tsinghua University. He attained his Ph.D. from Wuhan University of Technology in 2020. The main theme of Dr. Qi’s research is to study structure/microstructure property relations in functional ceramics. Email: qijunlei@whut.edu.cn

Yiying Chen, is a PhD candidate at the School of Materials Science and Engineering, Tsinghua University. Her research interests include microwave dielectric ceramics and capacitive energy storage performance enhancement.

Jinchen Qin, is a postdoctoral fellow in Shanghai Institute of Ceramics, Chinese Academy of Sciences (SICCAS). He attained his Ph.D. degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2023. His research focuses on AI-driven materials design, microwave dielectric ceramics, and low temperature cofired ceramics.

Ruoyi Lv, is a graduate student in the School of Materials Science and Engineering at Henan Polytechnic University. She graduated the Computer Science Department of Jiangxi Normal University in 2024. Her research focuses on thermoelectric materials based on machine-learning and python web programming (PWP).

Bin Wei, is an assistant professor in the School of Materials Science and Engineering at Henan Polytechnic University and currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow with Prof. Yuan-Hua Lin at Tsinghua University, China. He received his Ph.D. degree from Beijing Institute of Technology, China in 2020. He was a short-term scholar with Prof. Chen Li at University of California Riverside, USA in 2019. His research interests include lattice dynamics, thermoelectric materials, machine-learning, etc. Email: binwei@hpu.edu.cn

Zhifu Liu, is a full professor at Shanghai Institute of Ceramics, Chinese Academy of Sciences (SICCAS), where he earned his Ph.D. in 2004. From 2004-2006, he was a Research Scientist at Accelergy R&D Center (Shanghai). He then worked as a Postdoc Research Fellow at University of Toyama (2006-2008) and National Institute of Advanced Industry Science and Technology (AIST), Tsukuba (2008-2010). He joined SICCAS in April 2010. Now he is the leader of the Passive Integrated Devices and Material Group and serves as deputy director of the Information Functional Materials and Devices Research Center. He was selected as Shanghai Subject Chief Scientist in 2023. His research focuses on advanced dielectric materials, passive integration technology, sensing materials, and AI-driven materials design.

Yuan-Hua Lin, is a Changjiang Scholar Distinguished Professor and Dean of the School of Materials Science and Engineering, at Tsinghua University. He graduated from the Department of Materials Science and Engineering of Tsinghua University and got his Ph.D. degree, in 2001, and joined the Department of Physics at the University of Tokyo (funded by JSPS) as a postdoc in 2006. The main theme of Prof. Lin's research is high-performance dielectric materials, thermoelectric materials, and functional devices, etc. Prof. Lin has authored more than 400 papers in Science, Nature, Nature Materials, Nature Energy, PRL, etc. , with H-index of 100 and total citation > 35,000. He has given 80 plenary, keynote, and invited talks/seminars.
